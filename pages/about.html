About Us

Learn about our journey, values, and expertise

Our Story

A Legacy of Innovation and Quality

Founded in 2005, Massa began as a small workshop with a big vision - to revolutionize the brewing and extraction equipment industry with innovative, high-quality solutions that elevate product quality while improving operational efficiency.

What started as a team of three passionate engineers has grown into a global company with over 200 employees across three continents. Throughout our growth, we've maintained our commitment to craftsmanship, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, Massa equipment can be found in leading breweries, coffee roasteries, and botanical extraction facilities around the world. Our systems are renowned for their precision engineering, durability, and the exceptional quality of the end products they help create.

Our Mission

Driving Innovation in Extraction Technology

At Massa, our mission is to empower businesses in the beverage and extraction industries with state-of-the-art equipment that enhances product quality, improves operational efficiency, and drives sustainable growth.

We believe that exceptional equipment leads to exceptional products. That's why we invest heavily in research and development, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in brewing and extraction technology.

We're committed to supporting our clients throughout their journey - from initial consultation and custom design to installation, training, and ongoing technical support. Your success is our success.
